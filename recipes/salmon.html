Salmon

Description

Fried salmon served with microwave rice and boiled frozen vegetables. A quick and balanced meal that feels healthy and doesn't take much effort to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Salmon
  • Microwave rice
  • Frozen vegetables
  • Teriyaki stir fry sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in a pan on medium-low heat
  2. Place salmon in pan, cover in teriyaki sauce
  3. Cook for 14 minutes, turning halfway
  4. Place frozen vegetables in boiling water, boil for 5 minutes
  5. Tear the top of the rice package and microwave for 90 seconds
  6. Plate and serve
