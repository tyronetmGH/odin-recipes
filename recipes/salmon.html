Salmon

Description

Fried salmon served with microwave rice and boiled frozen vegetables. A quick and balanced meal that feels healthy and doesn't take much effort to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Fresh or frozen salmon
  • Microwave rice
  • Frozen vegetables
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Season salmon, thaw if frozen
  2. Place salmon in a pan in oil on medium heat for 14 minutes, turning halfway
  3. Place frozen vegetables in boiling water, boil for 5 minutes
  4. Tear the top of the rice and microwave for 90 seconds
  5. Plate and serve
