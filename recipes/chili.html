Chili

Description

A thick, hearty mix of ground beef, beans, corn, and salsa cooked slowly until everything blends together. It's warm, filling, and easy to make with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 large container of mild salsa
  • 1 can of corn
  • 2 cans of mixed beans
  • Ground Beef
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Add the salsa, corn, beans and meat to a slowcooker in layers
  2. Mix it all up and season to taste
  3. Set slowcooker heat to high and cook for 8 hours
  4. Set to slowcooker to warm and serve
