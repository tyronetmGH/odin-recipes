Chili

Description

Big yumyum time! Oooh I can't wait for chili in my tummy!

Ingredients

  • 1 large container of mild salsa
  • 1 can of corn
  • 2 cans of mixed beans
  • A nice chunk o' meat
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Add the salsa, corn, beans and meat to a slowcooker in layers
  2. Mix it all up and season to taste
  3. Set slowcooker heat to high and cook for 8 hours
