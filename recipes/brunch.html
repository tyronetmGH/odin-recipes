Brunch

Description

A basic plate with eggs, hashbrowns, and sausages. "Brunch" comes from a combination of breakfast and lunch, but I say why stop there?!

Ingredients

  • Sausages
  • Eggs
  • Hashbrowns
  • Seasoning
    • 2 1/2 tbsp paprika
    • 2 tbsp salt
    • 2 tbsp garlic powder
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 1 tbsp thyme
    • 1 tbsp black pepper
    • 1 tbsp onion powder
    • 1 tbsp cayenne powder

Steps

  1. Put oil in 3 saucepans and place 2 on medium heat
  2. Once heated, add hashbrowns to one pan and sausages to the other
  3. Sprinkle seasoning across hashbrowns and set a 14 minute timer
  4. Scramble eggs, adding salt, pepper, and 1 spoonful of milk and water for each egg
  5. Place 3rd pan on medium-low heat
  6. Turn sausages and mix the hashbrowns
  7. Pour the eggs into 3rd pan, mixing consistently for 5 minutes
  8. Serve while hot!
